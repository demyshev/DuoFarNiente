About Us

Nadezhda Skokova

Nadezhda is a musician with over 25 years of experience in solo, orchestral and chamber music. She earned her master's degree from the Moscow Conservatory and performed with multiple symphony orchestras, including 5 years as an artist of the Moscow Philharmonic. She has participated in numerous festivals, concerts and master classes worldwide.

Anton Demyshev

Anton has over 20 years of experience in orchestral, chamber, and solo performance. He studied at a prestigious music academy in Russia and toured internationally across Europe, Asia, and the U.S. After performing with the Moscow Philharmonic, he formed Duo Far Niente in the Bay Area.

FAQ

How to book us?

Reach out via WhatsApp, Instagram, or email at duofarniente@gmail.com. We request a $100 deposit to secure the date.

What is in your repertoire?

You can review our current playlist for solo and duo performances - View Playlist

Can I request a special song?

Yes. Let us know at least 1 week before the event.

Do you travel?

We perform within the U.S. For Bay Area events, there is no additional fee. For other states, travel and accommodation must be covered.

No speakers at the venue?

Not a problem. We bring battery-powered speakers and lights, so we can perform anywhere.
